Car Rent with Driver Terms and Conditions


1. Package Inclusions

- The rental package includes the use of the vehicle, fuel, road toll fees, and the services of a designated driver.

2. Package Exclusions

- Should the rental period exceed the allotted hours, an additional hourly fee shall apply.

3. Payment Terms

- CarRental accepts payments via over-the-counter cash transactions and GCash. Any additional costs incurred during the rental period shall be settled directly with the assigned driver.

4. No-Show Policy

- If the renter fails to appear within three (3) hours of the scheduled pick-up time, the reservation shall be deemed canceled without refund or compensation.

5. Cleaning Fees

- Any client-related mess, including but not limited to vomiting, spilling of liquids, or defecation, that requires special cleaning shall be subject to a penalty fee of PHP 500.

6. Liability for Damages

- The renter shall bear full financial responsibility for any damage caused to the vehicle during the rental period. This includes but is not limited to the full cost of repairs, restoration, and any revenue losses resulting from the vehicle being unavailable for future rentals.

7. Identification Requirements

- The renter must provide at least one (1) valid government-issued identification document, such as a driver’s license, passport, or national ID.

- By proceeding with the rental, the renter acknowledges and agrees to the terms and conditions outlined in this agreement.

- Payment will be collected on the scheduled pickup date.
